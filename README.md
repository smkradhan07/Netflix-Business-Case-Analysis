## Business Problem
Netflix wants to understand:
- What type of content to produce?
- Which countries to focus on?
- Whether to invest more in Movies or TV Shows?
- What content trends drive growth and retention?

## Dataset Information

- ~8800+ titles
- Movies and TV Shows
- Features include: cast, director, country, release year, rating, duration, genre

## Key Insights
- Majority content post 2015
- TV Shows drive retention
- Mature audience focus increasing
- Strong regional specialization

## Strategic Recommendations

1. Increase investment in TV Shows as they improve long-term user retention.
2. Focus on regional content production (India, Japan, South Korea) to expand market share.
3. Continue producing mature audience (TV-MA, TV-14) content which dominates demand.
4. Release major titles during high-content months (Q3/Q4) to maximize visibility.
